Fair and Intelligent Embedded System Challenge at ESWEEK 2023(current)

Introduction

Fairness competition track in Tiny and Fair ML Design contest at ESWEEK is a challenging, multi-month, research and development competition, focusing on real-world problems that require the implementation of machine learning algorithms on low-end micro-processors/microcontrollers. It is open to multi-person teams worldwide. The top three teams will be invited to the 2023 ESWEEK to present their solutions and to receive their awards.
